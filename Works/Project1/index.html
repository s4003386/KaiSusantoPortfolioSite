> About this project:

'Untitled' is a small 3d environment designed after brutalist liminal landscapes. The player is invited to explore by their own volition in a perpetual 'liminal' space to nowhere. The environment is designed after my own emotions going through life, where sometimes it can feel like there is no real end goal.

This video showcases a playthrough of the project.
